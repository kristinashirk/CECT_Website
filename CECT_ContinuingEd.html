Continuing Education

Continuing Education (CE) students are lifelong learners, whether they be emerging, incumbent, advancing, transitioning in the workforce. CE learners strive for success and personal growth through learning.

For example, if someone is early in their career, learners often take CE classes or earn particular certificates to augment what they learned in college and as a way to build professional acumen and skills as a way to help them to stand out in the workplace.

Or, if someone is new to the country and needs a way to quickly build skills and get a job, they might turn to a CE certificate program.

Many of our programs and certificates are designed to help people with knowledge, skills and abilities in one area transition to another industry or work role.
